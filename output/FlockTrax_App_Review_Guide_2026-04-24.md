# FlockTrax App Review Guide

Date: 2026-04-24
App: FlockTrax Mobile
Version: 1.0.1 (5)

## App Review Notes

FlockTrax Mobile is the mobile field-collection companion to the FlockTrax web console for poultry and farm operations teams. The review account is intentionally limited: reviewers can sign in, navigate the app, and evaluate the main read and field-collection flows, but some actions that would modify sensitive or production-linked operational data may be restricted. This is expected behavior and not a defect. The mobile app supports on-site data collection and operational record entry, while broader administrative and reporting workflows are handled in the FlockTrax web console.

## Platform Overview

FlockTrax is an operational platform for poultry and farm teams. It includes a web console for administration, reporting, and broader management, plus a mobile app for field use and day-to-day data collection.

## Intended Audience

FlockTrax is intended for authorized internal users such as farm managers, field staff, and operations personnel responsible for flock recordkeeping and operational tracking.

## What The Reviewer Should Expect

- Sign in with the supplied review credentials.
- Navigate the main mobile screens without navigation restrictions.
- Review representative field and record screens.
- Expect some write actions to be limited to protect live operational data.

## Why The Account Is Limited

The review account is configured to allow meaningful product evaluation while protecting production-linked data integrity.
