# FlockTrax Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Overview

FlockTrax is a business application used by authorized poultry field and operations personnel to review flock placement information and record operational data such as daily logs, mortality, weights, and feed delivery information.

This draft is intended as a starting point for the public privacy policy page needed for Google Play submission. It should be reviewed by the business owner before publication.

## Information We Collect

FlockTrax may collect and process the following categories of information:

- account information, such as your email address and internal user account identifier
- authentication information needed to sign you in and maintain your session
- operational data that you enter into the app, such as daily flock observations, mortality counts, cull notes, weight data, feed ticket information, and related comments
- technical information required to operate the service securely and reliably

FlockTrax does not appear to request device GPS location from the Android app at this time.

## How We Use Information

We use information collected through FlockTrax to:

- authenticate authorized users
- provide access to flock placement records and assigned operational workflows
- save field-entered operational data
- support account administration and security
- provide app functionality, including weather context for farm locations associated with placement records

## Data Sharing

FlockTrax does not sell personal information.

FlockTrax may use service providers and infrastructure necessary to operate the app, including backend hosting and related technical services. Information is shared only as needed to operate, secure, and support the app.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, unless a shorter retention period is required by law or contract.

## Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information processed through FlockTrax. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, but we work to protect data in transit and at rest where applicable.

## Account Deletion

If you have a FlockTrax account, you may request deletion of your account from within the mobile app using the in-app `Delete Account` option, when available for your account and environment.

If you are unable to access the app and need help with account deletion, contact the support email or support page listed below.

## Your Choices

You may:

- review and update certain information through your organization or administrator
- request account deletion through the app
- contact us with privacy-related questions

## Children

FlockTrax is intended for business and operational use by authorized personnel and is not directed to children.
